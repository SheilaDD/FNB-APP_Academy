Catwoman

About Catwoman

Catwoman is a comic character, a wily and agile professional thief and sometime love interest of superhero Batman.As deadly as she is beautiful, infamous cat-burglar Selina Kyle uses her nine lives to walk the razor's edge between light and darkness in Gotham City. The Bat may be the king of the beasts of Gotham. But the Cat? The Cat is definitely the queen. Catwoman has been baffling Gotham City's heroes, both masked and unmasked, from the very beginning.

Catwoman strength

  • Manipulative personality
