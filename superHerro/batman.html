Batman

About Batman

He is extremely pessimistic and suspicious, which often makes it difficult for him to trust people other than Alfred, Commissioner Gordon, Lucius Fox, the Robins, or the Batgirls. Despite this, Batman has proved to have a great love for humanity, which was instilled by his parents.

Batman Strength

  • World's Greatest Detective
  • Defeat supervillains twice his physical strength and or size
  • Reflexes
  • Speed
  • Master Hacker
